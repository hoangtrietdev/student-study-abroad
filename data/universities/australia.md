## General Notes for Australia
- **Visa**: Student Visa (subclass 500). Processing: 4–6 weeks online.
- **Cost of Living**: Sydney/Melbourne: AUD $21,000–$27,000/year; Other cities: AUD $18,000–$22,000/year
- **Part-time Work**: Up to 48 hours/fortnight during semester on Student Visa.
- **Post-study Work Visa**: Temporary Graduate Visa (subclass 485), 2–4 years depending on degree level.
- **OSHC**: Overseas Student Health Cover required (AUD $600–$900/year).
- **Application**: Direct to university. No central system like UCAS.
