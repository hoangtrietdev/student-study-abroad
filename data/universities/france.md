# Top Universities in France

## 1. Paris Sciences et Lettres (PSL University)
- **QS World Rank**: #24
- **Location**: Paris, Île-de-France
- **Type**: Public Research University (confederation)
- **Tuition (Undergrad — International)**: €170 (EU) / ~€2,770–€3,770/year (non-EU)
- **Tuition (Postgrad — International)**: €243 (EU) / ~€3,770/year (non-EU)
- **Acceptance Rate**: ~5% (highly selective grandes écoles)
- **Popular Programs**: Mathematics, Chemistry, Humanities, Biology, Art & Design (ENSAD)
- **Language Requirements**: IELTS 6.5–7.0+ / TOEFL 90+ (for English programs); French B2–C1
- **Scholarships**:
  - Eiffel Excellence Scholarship (French government, €1,181/month + benefits)
  - PSL Fellowship (doctoral research)
  - Erasmus+ for EU students
- **Notable**: Confederation of elite institutions including ENS Paris, MINES Paris, ESPCI. Extraordinary research output for its size.
- **Website**: https://psl.eu

## 2. Sorbonne University
- **QS World Rank**: #59
- **Location**: Paris, Île-de-France
- **Type**: Public Research University
- **Tuition (Undergrad — International)**: €170 (EU) / ~€2,770/year (non-EU)
- **Tuition (Postgrad — International)**: €243–€601 (EU) / ~€3,770/year (non-EU)
- **Acceptance Rate**: ~25%
- **Popular Programs**: Arts & Humanities, Literature, Medicine, Science, Languages
- **Language Requirements**: French B2–C1 required for most programs; some master's in English (IELTS 6.5+)
- **Scholarships**:

- **Notable**: One of world's oldest universities (est. 1150). Strong in humanities and sciences. Low tuition is a major advantage.
- **Website**: https://www.sorbonne-universite.fr

## 3. Institut Polytechnique de Paris (École Polytechnique)
- **QS World Rank**: #65
- **Location**: Palaiseau, Île-de-France
- **Type**: Public Grand École (Engineering)
- **Tuition (Undergrad — International)**: ~€15,000/year (Bachelor X)
- **Tuition (Postgrad — International)**: €0–€15,000/year (many fully funded for PhD)
- **Acceptance Rate**: <5% (extremely competitive)
- **Popular Programs**: Applied Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science, Economics, Data Science
- **Language Requirements**: French C1 (for X cycle) / English B2+ (for master's programs)
- **Scholarships**:
  - Polytechnique Scholarship (for international excellence students, partial funding)
  - Eiffel Excellence Scholarship
  - CNES/DGA Military-linked Research Scholarships
- **Notable**: France's most prestigious engineering school. Many French CEOs and politicians are X alumni. Strong military tradition.
- **Website**: https://www.polytechnique.edu

## 4. École Normale Supérieure Paris (ENS)
- **QS World Rank**: #41 (subject rankings: extremely high)
- **Location**: Paris, 5th arrondissement
- **Type**: Public Grand École (Research)
- **Tuition**: Essentially free for admitted students (normaliens receive a salary!)
- **Acceptance Rate**: <2% (one of France's most selective)
- **Popular Programs**: Mathematics, Physics, Philosophy, Biology, Literature
- **Language Requirements**: French C2 for normaliens; some English master's (IELTS 7.0+)
- **Scholarships**:
  - Normalien Monthly Allowance (~€1,300/month — students are paid civil servants!)
  - International Selection Programme (international students get similar support)
- **Notable**: Produces more Nobel Prize and Fields Medal winners per capita than any other institution. Admission is via intensely competitive nationwide exams.
- **Website**: https://www.ens.psl.eu

## 5. HEC Paris

- **Type**: Private Grande École (Business)
- **Tuition (Master in Management)**: ~€46,500 total
- **Tuition (MBA)**: ~€89,500 total
- **Acceptance Rate**: ~12%
- **Popular Programs**: Grande École (MiM), MBA, Executive MBA, PhD in Management
- **Language Requirements**: IELTS 7.5+ / TOEFL 105+ (MBA); GMAT 650+
- **Scholarships**:
  - HEC Paris Foundation Scholarships (merit + need)
  - Women's Full-Tuition Scholarship
  - HEC Alumni Foundation Awards
- **Notable**: #1 Business School in continental Europe. HEC MBA consistently ranked top 5 globally. Strong luxury, consulting, finance careers.
- **Website**: https://www.hec.edu

## 6. Sciences Po Paris
- **QS World Rank**: Top 100 in Social Sciences
- **Location**: Paris, 7th arrondissement
- **Type**: Public Grand École (Social Sciences)
- **Tuition (Undergrad — International)**: Sliding scale €0–€13,500/year (income-based)
- **Tuition (Postgrad — International)**: ~€12,000–€17,000/year
- **Acceptance Rate**: ~10% for international program
- **Popular Programs**: Political Science, International Affairs, Sociology, Economics, Law
- **Language Requirements**: French B2 + English B2 (bilingual program)
- **Scholarships**:
  - Sciences Po Scholarships (income-based, significant reduction)
  - Émile Boutmy Scholarship (merit-based for non-EU, up to €14,100/year)
  - Eiffel Excellence Scholarship
- **Notable**: Produces most French Presidents, prime ministers, and diplomats. International Programs taught 50/50 French-English.
- **Website**: https://www.sciencespo.fr

## General Notes for France
- **Visa**: Long Stay Student Visa (VLS-TS). Apply via Campus France.
- **Cost of Living**: Paris €1,200–€1,800/month; Other cities €800–€1,200/month
- **Part-time Work**: Up to 964 hours/year (equivalent to 20 hrs/week) on student visa.
- **Language**: Most undergraduate programs in French. Growing number of master's in English.
- **Healthcare**: French social security covers international students (basic coverage ~€11/month with CVEC contribution).
- **CVEC**: Campus Life Contribution — ~€103/year mandatory fee.
- **Application**: Via Campus France portal (Etudes en France). Deadlines vary Dec–March.
