## 1. National University of Singapore (NUS)
- **QS World Rank**: #8
- **Location**: Kent Ridge, Singapore
- **Type**: National Autonomous Research University
- **Tuition (Singapore PR/Citizens)**: SGD $8,000–$17,550/year (subsidized)
- **Tuition (International — with subsidy)**: SGD $17,550–$31,450/year (~$13,000–$23,300/year)
- **Tuition (International — without subsidy)**: SGD $38,700–$71,900/year
- **Acceptance Rate**: ~7–15% for international students
- **Popular Programs**: Computer Science, Engineering, Medicine, Law, Business (NUS Business School)
- **Language Requirements**: IELTS 6.0–7.0+ / TOEFL 85–100+
- **Scholarships**:
  - NUS Global Merit Scholarship (full tuition + allowance + accommodation)
  - ASEAN Undergraduate Scholarship (full tuition + living allowance + airfare)
  - Science & Technology Undergraduate Scholarship
  - NUS-MOE Scholarship (for tuition grant commitment to work in Singapore 3 years)
- **Notable**: Asia's #1 or #2 university (alternates with NTU). World #8 overall. Extremely strong in Computer Science, Engineering, and Business. English-medium teaching. Strategic location in Asia.
- **Website**: https://www.nus.edu.sg

## 2. Nanyang Technological University (NTU)
- **QS World Rank**: #26
- **Location**: Jurong West, Singapore
- **Type**: National Autonomous Research University
- **Tuition (International — with subsidy)**: SGD $17,000–$30,000/year (~$12,600–$22,200/year)
- **Tuition (International — without subsidy)**: SGD $38,000–$65,000/year
- **Acceptance Rate**: ~15–20%
- **Popular Programs**: Engineering, Business (Nanyang MBA), Art, Design & Media, Computer Science, Materials Science
- **Language Requirements**: IELTS 6.0+ / TOEFL 85+
- **Scholarships**:
  - NTU ASEAN Scholarship (full tuition + allowance + hostel)
  - Nanyang Scholarship (Singaporean students mainly but open to all)
  - NTU MOE Tuition Grant (international students sign 3-year Singapore work bond)
  - NTU PhD Scholarships (full funding for research degrees)
- **Notable**: Ranked #1 globally for young universities (under 50 years). Beautiful eco-friendly campus. World-class in AI research, Artificial Intelligence Research Institute (AISG).
- **Website**: https://www.ntu.edu.sg

## General Notes for Singapore
- **Visa**: Student's Pass (STP). Apply via SOLAR (Student's Pass On-Line Application & Registration). Processing: 2–4 weeks.
- **Cost of Living**: SGD 1,500–2,500/month (~$1,100–$1,850/month). Expensive but very safe.
- **Part-time Work**: Up to 16 hrs/week during term with Student's Pass from MOE-registered institutions.
- **Tuition Grant (MOE)**: International students who accept the subsidy commit to work in Singapore for 3 years post-graduation — this makes it affordable. Great deal for those wanting to work in Singapore.
- **Language**: English is the medium of instruction everywhere. Official languages: English, Mandarin, Malay, Tamil.
- **Safety & Quality of Life**: Singapore is ranked #1 in Asia for safety and quality of life. Extremely clean, efficient, and well-organized.
- **Connectivity**: Major international hub — direct flights globally. Gateway to Southeast Asia.
