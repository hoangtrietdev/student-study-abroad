## 1. ETH Zurich (Swiss Federal Institute of Technology)
- **QS World Rank**: #7
- **Location**: Zurich
- **Type**: Public Federal Research University
- **Tuition (Undergrad — All students)**: CHF 730/semester (~CHF 1,460/year) 🎉
- **Tuition (Postgrad — Master's)**: CHF 730/semester (~CHF 1,460/year)
- **Acceptance Rate**: ~27% (bachelor's); selective for international master's
- **Popular Programs**: Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Architecture, Mathematics, Physics
- **Language Requirements**: German B2–C1 (undergrad); IELTS 7.0+ / TOEFL 100+ (English master's programs)
- **Scholarships**:
  - ETH Excellence Scholarship & Opportunity Programme (master's, CHF 12,000/semester + fee waiver)
  - Excellence Scholarship for PhD (ETH Zurich Graduate Fellowships)
  - Swiss Government Excellence Scholarship (ESKAS)
- **Notable**: One of the lowest tuition fees for a world-class university. 21 Nobel Prize laureates. Einstein studied and taught here.
- **Website**: https://www.ethz.ch

## 2. EPFL (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne)
- **QS World Rank**: #17
- **Location**: Lausanne, Vaud
- **Type**: Public Federal Research University
- **Tuition (All students)**: CHF 730/semester (~CHF 1,460/year)
- **Acceptance Rate**: ~17% for master's (highly selective)
- **Popular Programs**: Life Sciences Engineering, Computer Science, Architecture, Electrical Engineering, Robotics
- **Language Requirements**: French B2 (undergrad); English B2–C1 / IELTS 7.0+ (master's in English)
- **Scholarships**:
  - EPFL Excellence Fellowships (master's, CHF 10,000/semester)
  - EPFL Doctoral School Fellowship
  - Swiss Government Excellence Scholarship (ESKAS)
  - Erna Hamburger Prize (women in STEM)
- **Notable**: Sister institution to ETH Zurich. Renowned for robotics, life sciences, and clean energy research. Located on Lake Geneva.
- **Website**: https://www.epfl.ch

## General Notes for Switzerland
- **Visa**: Schengen Student Visa + Swiss residence permit (B permit). Apply via Swiss Embassy.
- **Cost of Living**: ~CHF 2,000–2,500/month (one of the most expensive countries in the world)
- **Part-time Work**: Up to 15 hrs/week during semester. Full-time during holidays.
- **Language**: Four official languages: German, French, Italian, Romansh. City determines primary language.
- **Swiss Government Excellence Scholarship (ESKAS)**: Prestigious fully-funded government scholarship for international students — highly recommended to apply.
- **CERN**: Many ETH/EPFL/Geneva students get research opportunities at CERN (particle physics lab).
