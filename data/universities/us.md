## 1. Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)
- **QS World Rank**: #1
- **Location**: Cambridge, Massachusetts
- **Type**: Private Research University
- **Tuition (Undergrad)**: ~$59,750/year
- **Tuition (Postgrad)**: ~$60,000–$80,000/year
- **Acceptance Rate**: ~4%
- **Popular Programs**: Computer Science, Engineering, Physics, Economics, Architecture
- **Language Requirements**: IELTS 7.0+ / TOEFL 100+
- **Scholarships**:
  - MIT Scholarship (need-based, average $55,000/year — no loans policy)
  - MIT Presidential Fellowship (graduate students)
  - NSF Graduate Research Fellowship
- **Notable**: Families earning <$140k/year may qualify for fully funded need-based aid. No student loans in financial aid packages.
- **Website**: https://www.mit.edu

## General Notes for USA
- **Visa**: F-1 Student Visa required. Processing: 2–4 months before start date.
- **Cost of Living**: $15,000–$25,000/year depending on city
- **Part-time Work**: On-campus up to 20 hrs/week on F-1 visa. OPT allows 1 year post-grad work.
- **Language Tests**: Most require IELTS 6.5–7.5 or TOEFL 90–110
- **Application Deadlines**: Regular Decision Jan 1–Feb 1; Early Decision/Action Nov 1–Nov 15
